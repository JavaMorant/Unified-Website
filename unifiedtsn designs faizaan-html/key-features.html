Key Features Unified TSN is a social networking platform for students, graduates, and tutors. We understand the challenges and stresses that come with university life, and that's why we've developed exclusive features to help enhance your experience.
Swipe & Match "Swipe and match" feature allows users to quickly browse through potential matches and express interest by swiping right or left, creating a mutually interested connection.
PSBs Unified TSN has introduced a new feature called PSBs (purpose-specific bios) that can be added to user profiles to help find friends or coursemates who have similar interests and intentions on the app.
Marketplace as a Shopper In our marketplace, browse through & shop different products and services all created and managed by your fellow students.
Business page as an Entrepreneur The marketplace for all student entrepreneurs. Upload your products & services, add links to your websites & socials, interact with potential customers and partner with other student brands!
Tutors ‘Chat’ Via the chat feature, tutors are able to answer questions from students & help them with their academic difficulties, unlike anywhere else, tutors are paid to chase their own leads and do not pay a commission!
Tutors ‘Resources’ Your resources are your comprehensive collection of learning materials you can upload anytime you want for students to download. They may include articles, videos, presentations, mock exams, webinars etc.
